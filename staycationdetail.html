Umaid Lake Palace – An Organic Retreat

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and head to Umaid Lake Palace – An Organic Retreat, a charming resort set in the alluring countryside of Rajasthan. Overlooking tranquil Kalakho Lake and garlanded by a Mughal-style garden, this palatial 20-acre resort was once a royal hunting lodge. A regal sanctuary perfect for a weekend escape, Umaid Lake Palace is a family-run resort that promises heartwarming hospitality and personalised service. All food served at the resort is grown and sourced locally, with an emphasis on fresh, healthy and organic cuisine. The dishes are flavoured with herbs and spices from the onsite garden, adding a wholesome, authentic Rajasthani touch. Spend time at the Umaid farm and dairy and learn about everyday life on the land, maybe even trying your hand at harvesting and cooking crops. Your handpicked inclusions: Step into the lush resort and be welcomed with a refreshing non-alcoholic drink Begin each day with authentic farm-to-table breakfast End the day with one pint of beer per adult (one pint per person, per day) Craving more deliciousness? Enjoy a 15% discount on food and soft drinks Make the most of your stay with unique experiences: one guided tour of the in-house organic farm and dairy per stay, one sunrise or sunset cycling excursion per stay, one morning or evening nature walk per stay, and one evening arati ceremony at the in-house temple per stay Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi Travelling with family? One child (7-12) or two infants (six and under) stay free in existing bedding with all inclusions (except alcohol).
